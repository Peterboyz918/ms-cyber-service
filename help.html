How MS Cyber Works

Follow these steps to complete your order successfully

1

Browse & Select Service

Navigate to the Services tab. Choose your preferred package (Printing, Research, or Cyber Access) and click "Add to Order".

2

Verify Order Details

Go to Order Summary. Check the pricing and your M-Pesa phone number. Ensure everything is correct before moving to payment.

3

Make Payment

Click "Checkout". Wait for the M-Pesa STK push on your phone. Enter your PIN. Your status will update to Paid immediately.

4

Download Results

Once completed, go to My Orders. You can download your documents or view your session receipts directly.
